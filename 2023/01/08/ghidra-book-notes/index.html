编写Ghidra脚本

原则1：在编写脚本之前需要看原本的脚本库里面有没有，如果没有才开始写

添加extentions ：将extentions文件放在Extensions\Ghidra文件夹下面，然后直接在Ghidra中点击File | Install
Extensions来安装扩展。

When developing, you must first decide what kind of project you are dealing with: analyzer, plugin, loader, filesystem, or exporter.

The analyzer’s type can be one of the following: BYTE_ANALYZER, DATA_ANALYZER, FUNCTION_ANALYZER, FUNCTION_MODIFIERS_ANALYZER,FUNCTION_SIGNATURES_ANALYZER, INSTRUCTION_ANALYZER, or ONE_SHOT_ANALYZER.
